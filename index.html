About Us

Himalayan Pheasant 2.0 is a Prototype concept battery-electric driven automobile. It is an augmented form of Himalayan Pheasant 1.0 that represented Nepal in Shell Eco-Marathon 2021 and secured 127th position making it a proud moment to celebrate. Inspired and driven by the enthusiasm and commitment of Himalayan Pheasant 1.0, we are a team of multidisciplinary students working full-fledgedly to meet our vision and mission i.e to contribute to a sustainable future by building a vehicle that can travel more distance with a certain volume of fuel.
